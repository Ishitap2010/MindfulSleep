From 5 Hours to 8.5 Hours: My Sleep Transformation Journey

When I started using Mindful Sleep, I was averaging 5 hours of sleep and my GPA was 2.3. I thought sleeping was for lazy people. After learning about neuroplasticity and memory consolidation, everything changed. I gradually shifted my bedtime earlier, created a wind-down routine, and optimized my study schedule around my sleep needs.

The results were incredible: My GPA jumped from 2.3 to 3.5 in just one semester! I feel more focused, less anxious, and actually enjoy learning now. The sleep science modules helped me understand WHY sleep matters, and the community kept me motivated during tough moments.

Key insight: Sleep is when your brain files away what you learned
Main strategy: Consistent bedtime, even on weekends
Best part: Feeling proud of taking care of myself
